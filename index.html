Why to invest in Baja

Oceanfront condos just minutes from the U.S. border

Located 25 min from San Ysidro and 40 min from CBX, THE WAVVE offers easy access from Southern California.

Secure legal ownership through trust

U.S. citizens can legally own beachfront property in Mexico through a bank trust (fideicomiso), or via a Mexican LLC or corporation. Both structures offer full rights to rent, resell, or inherit.

Higher value per square foot vs. Southern California

Baja offers 3–4x more space per dollar compared to Southern California, making it ideal for second homes and investment properties.

Baja California, Mexico — an emerging market for American investment

Baja attracts U.S. and Mexican-American buyers seeking legal, transferable, and affordable real estate options near the border.

Condos priced in USD for buyer clarity

Developments like THE WAVVE reduce currency risk and simplify valuation by pricing units in U.S. dollars.

Lower cost of living in Mexico

Cost of living — including housing, healthcare, and property taxes — is significantly lower than in the U.S., offering long-term financial advantages for buyers and residents.
